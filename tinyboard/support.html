Frequently Asked Questions

Why am I not seeing any arrivals?

TinyBoard shows transit data for New York City. Make sure your device location is set to an NYC area, or search for a specific stop by name or address.

How do I restore my subscription?

Open TinyBoard, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device to manage or cancel your TinyBoard Pro subscription.

Why are arrival times inaccurate?

TinyBoard displays real-time data from the MTA and other transit agencies. Occasionally, the source data may be delayed or inaccurate. Try pulling to refresh for the latest data.

Does TinyBoard work offline?

TinyBoard requires an internet connection to fetch real-time arrivals. Your saved stops and preferences are stored locally and will appear when you reopen the app, but arrival times need a network connection to update.

How do I add a widget?

Long-press your home screen, tap the "+" button in the top corner, search for "TinyBoard," and add the widget. You can configure it to show a specific saved stop or your nearest favorite.
